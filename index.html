WEDODILLODAY.

Mayfest Productions is a student organization at Northwestern University that plans and produces the nation's largest student-run music festival — Dillo Day, with over 12,000 festival attendees. In addition to the festival, Mayfest hosts and promotes a number of other programming events including Battle of the Bands and Battle of the DJs.

Dillo Day 53 tickets now available!

1

Acknowledge the Dillo Day event terms and conditions.

Read the Dillo Day Terms and Conditions before purchasing your tickets. Failure to abide by the terms may result in ticket revocation or removal from the event.

Read Terms and Conditions
2

Register for your wristbands.

Visit the Dillo Day 2025 event on NBO to register for your wristband by May 13, 2025. Northwestern undergraduate students may register for free. Northwestern undergraduate students can also choose to purchase 1 guest wristband. Northwestern graduate students, Evanston community members, and Northwestern alumni can choose to purchase a wristband. Please save the 6-character confirmation code from the order for distribution.

Order Wristbands
3

Pick up your wristbands before Dillo Day.

Northwestern undergraduate students, graduate students, faculty, staff, and Evanston residents must pick up their wristbands in person by May 16, 2025. A valid Wildcard or government-issued ID matching the name on the order must be presented along with your 6-character confirmation code. Northwestern undergraduate students and graduate students are also responsible for picking up their registered guest's wristband during distribution and must have a photo of the guest's government-issued ID available. See wristband distribution terms in the Dillo Day Terms and Conditions for more information.
